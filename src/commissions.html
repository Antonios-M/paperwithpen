1/2 + Refuge
Type: Gallery piece
Client: Via Architecture
Year: 2021
Location: North Point, Hong Kong
Size: 420 x 297 mm

Situated along the tramline of Hong Kong Island, the Novum East project demonstrates the possibility of an urban to rural coexistence in modern residential development. The detailed pen lining depicts the concept of an “urban barn”. The transition of scenery from the bustling street of North Point to the ridge of Braemar Hill reflects the diverse environment of the site.
